About Able Table

There has been a recent trend in America to hone in on healthy eating patterns and reduce the growing obesity problem in our population. It has been estimated that Americans spend $60 billion annually on weight loss products and services, as 108 million of us are on diets. What truly has inspired the founding of Able Table, however, does not lie in trends but rather in personal values and upbringing, loss and observation, and the desire to help people to stop dieting, take control of their lives and find balance and enjoyment in eating good food and living a full life. The food we put in our bodies has a huge impact on our health, our state of mind, and our well-being. The personal loss I experienced could have been avoided simply with good nutrition, healthy meals and a sustainable lifestyle. I have always enjoyed cooking and baking for others. Additionally, I have received many requests over the years from friends and colleagues, to help them eat better food. With those pieces in mind, I founded Able Table.

Able Table is a consultation and personal chef service that is focused on providing healthy, customized and sustainable meals to its clients. Able Table focuses on busy individuals and families working to achieve and maintain a healthy lifestyle. We provide convenience, customization and connections. Able Table focuses on meal preparation for clients striving to eat healthfully but also offers services such as general meal consultations, menu planning, grocery shopping, recipe adaptations and cooking lessons by request. With the goal of providing a realistic and holistic approach to healthy eating and healthy living, Able Table focuses on building relationships with clients, and not just offering one-size-fits-all plans and technologies like many current markets trends and competitors. Instead, Able Table works with each client on an individual basis to build a plan that is desirable and feasible, long-term. Able Table - giving you the ability to have healthy food at your table so you can eat well and live well.
